The Tesla Coil
Tesla's intriguing creation, the Tesla Coil, revealed Tesla's command of high-frequency electrical phenomena. This resonant transformer generated exceptionally high voltages and amazing electrical displays, enthralling audiences with its brilliant lightning-like discharges. While the Tesla Coil's original function was in wireless power transmission research, its relevance extends beyond its scientific accomplishments. It has become an iconic symbol of scientific curiosity, inspiring future generations while yet retaining a magical attraction.
